2.1.3 Birthday Rewards

1. "Birthday reward" is a great way to make customers feel special and appreciated, encouraging them to shop with you around their birthday. You can go birthday reward page as same like others edit button then you have to active this settings.

2. If you set 100 points as a birthday reward on his birthday date this customer auto get reward.

Example: Customer’s Birthday: X’s birthday is on April 10th. On April 10th, X automatically receives 100 bonus points in her loyalty account as a birthday gift from your store.

Tips for Setting Up Birthday Rewards:

  1. Set a generous but manageable number of points to make customers feel appreciated without affecting profitability.
  2. Promote the Birthday Reward feature on your loyalty page and through email campaigns to ensure customers provide their birth dates.
  3. Send a reminder or personalized email near the customer’s birthday to let them know about their reward.
  4. Combine birthday rewards with special birthday discounts or offers to further increase engagement and sales.
