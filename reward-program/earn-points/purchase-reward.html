2.1.2. Purchase Reward

1. After click this edit button you can find out purchase reward details.

2. You can change the reward name with this option, similar to the Sign-Up Reward feature.

3. The "Purchase Rewards" feature allows customers to earn points every time they make a purchase. You can set specific point values for different purchase amounts, encouraging repeat purchases and increasing customer loyalty.

Reward Options:

  • 1. Every $1 Spent = Points:

    Reward customers based on the amount they spend:

    • Example 1: If you set Every $1 Spent = 1 Point, a $10 purchase earns 10 points.
    • Example 2: If you set Every $1 Spent = 5 Points, a $10 purchase earns 50 points.

    This system directly links rewards to the amount spent, incentivizing higher spending.

  • 2. Fixed Amount of Points:

    Reward customers with a fixed number of points for each transaction, regardless of the purchase amount:

    • Example: If you set a fixed rule of 100 points per transaction, a $10 purchase earns 100 points, and a $50 purchase also earns 100 points.

    This system provides consistent rewards, encouraging frequent transactions rather than higher spending.

Tips for Setting Up Purchase Rewards:

  1. Use the "Every $1 Spent" option to encourage higher spending from customers while maintaining flexibility to adjust the points-to-currency ratio.
  2. Consider the "Fixed Amount of Points" option for low-ticket items or to reward frequent shoppers equally, regardless of purchase value.
  3. Test different reward structures to find what resonates most with your audienceâ€”adjust based on feedback and sales performance.
  4. Clearly communicate the rewards program on your loyalty page and at checkout to ensure customers understand the benefits.
  5. Monitor redemption rates to ensure your rewards program remains profitable while engaging your customers effectively.
  6. Use the Survey Program feature to ask customers what they like and need. Their feedback can help you improve your purchase rewards to make them more attractive and useful.
