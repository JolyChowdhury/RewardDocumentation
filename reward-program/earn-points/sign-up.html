Sign-up Reward

1. After click this edit button you can find out sign up rewards details.

2. Each earning point page has a toggle button to activate or deactivate specific programs.

  • Active:These are the rules that allow your customers to earn points and are visible on your widget or loyalty page.
  • Inactive: These rules do not allow customers to earn points and will be hidden from your widget or loyalty page.

3. You can change the reward name using this modal, and after updating it.

4. After change reward name you'll also see the summary reflect the new name.

5. With this option, you can set reward points to give your customers as a welcome reward and you'll also see the summary reflect the new points.

6. After completing the setup, you must click the Create button otherwise, your setup will not work.

Tips for Setting Up Sign-Up Points:

  1. Offer a generous but sustainable number of points to encourage sign-ups. For example, 100-200 points can be an attractive starting reward.
  2. Clearly display the reward points on your loyalty page to motivate new customers to join.
  3. Ensure the reward value aligns with your business model. Calculate the points-to-currency ratio to avoid over-rewarding.
  4. Promote your sign-up reward on social media and email campaigns to maximize engagement.
  5. Regularly review and adjust the reward points based on customer feedback and business performance.
