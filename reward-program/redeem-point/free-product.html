Free Product

The "Free Product" settings allow you to reward loyal customers by offering free products, such as a "Buy One Get One" offer.

How to Set It Up:

1. Set Reward Name : Provide a clear and descriptive name for the discount. This name should help you and your customers easily identify the purpose of the discount.

1. Points Cost: Specify the number of points a customer needs to redeem in order to claim a free shipping coupon. This is a required field; you must set the points value to proceed with the setup.

2. Choose the Discount Type: You can select one of the following discount types:

  • Fixed Amount Discount: Offer a specific monetary discount, such as $10 off a customer’s order. This discount applies as a fixed value, regardless of the order total.
  • Percentage Discount: Offer a discount as a percentage of the total order value. For example, a 10% discount on a $50 order will give the customer $5 off.

3. Choose Applicable Categories: Specify where the discount can be applied:

  • Specific Collection: After selecting a specific collection, click Browse. A modal will appear where you can view all the collections in your store. You can choose one or multiple collections to apply the discount to all products within the selected collections.
    1. Add or cancel your selected collections as needed.
    2. The selected collections will be displayed under the Choose Applicable Categories section.
    3. You can remove any selected collection if necessary.
    For example, you can offer a $10 discount on all products within a specific collection. If a fixed discount amount is selected, the discount will be applied to all eligible products in the chosen collection.
  • Specific Product: After selecting the "Specific Product" option, click Browse. A modal will appear where you can view all the products in your store, including their variants, prices, and availability. You can choose one or multiple products and their variants to apply the discount.
    1. Add or cancel your selected products as needed.
    2. The selected products will be displayed under the Choose Applicable Categories section.
    3. You can remove any selected product if necessary.
    4. You can also edit the selected products if needed. Clicking the Edit button will open a modal displaying the selected products and their variants (if applicable). Here, you can modify or deselect your chosen products.
    For example, you can set a 10% discount on specific products only, allowing targeted promotional offers for selected items.

Quick Tips for Free Product Rewards:

  1. Choose appealing products: Select popular or complementary items to encourage customers to redeem points.
  2. Promote BOGO deals: Offer “Buy One, Get One Free” on high-demand items to boost sales and loyalty.
  3. Target specific categories: Limit free product rewards to seasonal or surplus inventory to manage costs effectively.
