2.2.3. Free Product

The "Free Product" settings allow you to reward loyal customers by offering free products, such as a "Buy One Get One" offer.

How to Set It Up:

1. Discount Name: Create a unique name for your free product discount to make it easily identifiable.

2. Choose the Discount Type: You can select one of the following discount types:

  • Fixed Amount Discount: Offer a specific monetary discount, such as $10 off a customer’s order. This discount applies as a fixed value, regardless of the order total.
  • Percentage Discount: Offer a discount as a percentage of the total order value. For example, a 10% discount on a $50 order will give the customer $5 off.

3. Choose Applicable Categories: Specify where the discount can be applied:

  • Specific Collection: Apply the discount to an entire product collection. For example, offer $10 off all products within a specific collection. If a fixed amount is selected, this discount applies across all eligible products in the chosen collection.
  • Specific Product: Apply the discount to one or more selected products. For example, set a 10% discount on specific products only.

Quick Tips for Free Product Rewards:

  1. Choose appealing products: Select popular or complementary items to encourage customers to redeem points.
  2. Promote BOGO deals: Offer “Buy One, Get One Free” on high-demand items to boost sales and loyalty.
  3. Target specific categories: Limit free product rewards to seasonal or surplus inventory to manage costs effectively.
