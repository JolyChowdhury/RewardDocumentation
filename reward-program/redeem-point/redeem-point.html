Ways to Redeem Points

Click the Add Ways to Redeem button to explore all available redemption options.

The Redeem Points feature allows your customers to exchange their earned points for rewards. This not only encourages repeat purchases but also keeps customers engaged with your loyalty program. Our available redeeming programs include:

  1. Order Discount
  2. Free Shipping
  3. Free Product

Each option allows customers to redeem their points for valuable rewards, enhancing their shopping experience.

How customers redeem their reward?

Customers can go to Redeem Points in their account dashboard.

Customers will see a list of their available discount codes, benefits, and redemption options.

By clicking the Redeem button, customers can use their points to claim rewards such as discounts or other benefits.
