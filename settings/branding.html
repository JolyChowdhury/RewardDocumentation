Branding Customization

Make your loyalty widget match your store's branding by adjusting colors, text, icons, visibility, and button positioning.

To start customizing, go to Reward-Race Loyalty > Settings > Branding.

Widget Customization

On the left side, you can adjust all settings, while on the right side, you’ll see a live preview of your changes.

Theme Customization

Customize the look and feel of your widget with the following options:

  1. Widget Colors:
    1. Primary and Secondary Color: Adjust the gradient colors that will appear in the background, icons, and button background to create a more attractive widget for your store.
    2. Text Color: Change the header text color to align with your branding.
    3. Menu Text Color: Change the color of the menu text to match your store’s theme.
  2. Widget Visibility: Control when the widget is visible. You can hide or show the widget on mobile devices within your store.
  3. Widget Icon:
    1. Choose Button Icon: Select an existing icon or upload your own to appear on the widget header and button.
    2. Choose Reward Menu Icon: Upload or select an icon to represent the rewards menu.
    3. Choose Earn Menu Icon: Upload or choose an icon to represent the earn menu.
    4. Choose Redeem Menu Icon: Upload or choose an icon to represent the redeem menu.
    5. Choose Activity Menu Icon: Upload or choose an icon to represent the activity menu.
    6. Choose Referral Menu Icon: Upload or choose an icon to represent the referral menu.
  4. Widget Text: Customize various text elements in the widget:
    1. Header Text:
      1. Greeting Text: Modify the greeting message displayed at the top of the widget.
      2. Program Name: Change the program name shown on the widget.
    2. Button Text:
      1. Reward Button Text: Customize the text for the reward button.
      2. Earn Button Text: Edit the text for the earn button.
      3. Redeem Button Text: Edit the text for the redeem button.
      4. Recent Activities Button Text: Modify the text for the recent activities button.
      5. Referral Button Text: Change the text for the referral button.
  5. Widget Button: Customize the appearance of your widget’s button:
    1. Button Text: Adjust the text on the button to reflect your branding.
    2. Button Type:
      1. Icon and Text: Display both icon and text on the button.
      2. Icon Only: Display only the icon.
      3. Text Only: Display only the text.
    3. Button Position:
      1. Top Left: Place the button in the top-left corner of your screen.
      2. Top Right: Place the button in the top-right corner of your screen.
      3. Bottom Left: Place the button in the bottom-left corner of your screen.
      4. Bottom Right: Place the button in the bottom-right corner of your screen.
    4. Button Spacing:
      1. Side Spacing: Adjust the horizontal space from the left or right edges of the screen (e.g., 10px or 20px).
      2. Bottom Spacing: Adjust the vertical space from the top or bottom edges of the screen (e.g., 10px or 20px).
