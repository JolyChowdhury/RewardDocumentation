Survey

The Survey feature allows you to gather valuable customer feedback through customizable surveys. Follow these steps to create, manage, and analyze your surveys:

Getting Started

  1. Navigate to Reward-Race Loyalty > Survey.
  2. Explore the tools and options available for setting up surveys.

Survey Steps

Activate Survey Status

To start using surveys, activate the feature by clicking the "Turn On" button.

Create Survey

To create a new survey, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Create Survey" button.
  2. Your created survey list will be show here.
  3. You can see all of your survey list with View all .

The "Create Survey" feature helps you quickly design and deploy a survey to gather customer feedback. Follow these steps to get started:

Step 1: What Will Your Survey Say?

  1. Slide Template: Choose how to build your survey slides:
    • Build Manually: Create each slide from scratch with full customization.
    • Start with a Template: Use a pre-made template to save time. (Upcoming Feature)

Step 2: Where Will Your Survey Show Up?

  1. Display Rules: Select where the survey will be displayed on your website:
    • Web: Display the survey on specific web pages or conditions.
    • Every Page: Display the survey on all pages of your website (Upcoming Feature)

Step 3: Select a Display Template

  1. Display Template: Choose from available templates to determine how the survey will appear to users. Currently, the only available option is:
    • Product Page (Logged-in Customers): Display the survey on product pages for logged-in users.

    Note: Additional page options are not available yet but will be introduced as an upcoming feature.

Step 4: What Should Your Survey Be Named?

  1. Survey Title: Enter a descriptive name for your survey to easily identify it later.

Final Step: Create Your Survey

  1. Once all fields are filled out, click the "Create Survey" button to save and start configuring your survey.

Create Slide

Within each survey, you can create multiple slides to gather specific information. Each slide has various settings and options to tailor the survey experience. Here's a breakdown of the features:

  1. Click the "Create Slide" button.

Step 1: Select Survey

  1. Displays a dropdown of all the surveys you have created.
  2. Select the survey where this slide will be added.

Example: If you have a survey named "Customer Feedback Survey" select it from the dropdown to associate the slide with that survey.

Step 2: Format

Choose the format of the slide based on the type of question you want to ask. Available options include:

  1. Single Choice: Users can select one option.
  2. Multi Choice: Users can select multiple options.
  3. Binary: Yes/No or True/False type of question.
  4. Email Capture: Collect email addresses.
  5. Satisfaction Scale: Measure user satisfaction using emojis or scales.
  6. Star Rating: Rate something on a scale of stars (e.g., 1â€“5 stars).
  7. Short Answer: Open-ended question for users to type responses.

Example: If you want users to rate your product, select Star Rating.

Step 3: Content

Add the main content of the slide:

  1. Main Heading: The title of the slide.
  2. Subheading (Optional): Additional details or instructions for the question.

Example: Main Heading: "How would you rate our service?"

Step 4: Options

Displays information if you want to show:

  1. Left Label (Optional): Displays information on the left side, such as a description or rating category.
  2. Right Label (Optional): Displays information on the right side, such as a description or rating category.

Example:For a star rating widget, you can set the left label as "Bad" and the right label as "Good". This helps users understand the rating scale.

Step 5: Logic

Use logic to configure special behaviors based on the user's responses.

  1. Example Logic 1: If the user select "1/2 Stars" redirect them to a feedback slide to provide more details.
  2. Example Logic 2: If the user selects "5 Stars" then survey will be end.

Step 6: Settings

  1. Show Heading: Display or hide the slide title.
  2. Hidden: Hide the slide within the survey if necessary (e.g., for internal purposes or conditional display based on logic).

Final Step: Create Your Slide

  1. Once all fields are filled out, click the "Create Slide" button to save.

Slide Preview

  1. On the right side, you can see a live preview of the slide based on the settings you configure.
  2. This helps you visualize how the slide will look to users.
