Survey Report

View your survey performance and insights by clicking the "See survey reports" button.

On the report page, you will find several options to customize your view:

  1. Date Range: Use this option to select your preferred date range. You can choose:
    • A specific date
    • A longer range (e.g., January to March)
    • Quick options like "Yesterday" or "Last 7 Days"
  2. Survey Responses: This section allows you to select and review reports for specific surveys you have created. Choose the desired survey to analyze its performance.
  3. After configuring your settings, click the "Generate Report" button to view the results. The generated report will display all relevant insights and metrics.

Specific Survey Report

View detailed reports for each individual survey to gain specific insights.

From the survey list, you can access a survey report by clicking on the corresponding icon:

  1. View the total number of customer responses for the selected survey.
  2. Access a detailed breakdown of the specific survey's performance and insights.

Specific Slide Report

Access individual reports for every slide within a survey to analyze detailed responses.

From the slide list, you can view the report for a specific slide by clicking on the respective icon:

  1. Access a detailed overview of the selected slide's report.
  2. View the total number of customer responses for the selected slide.
  3. Navigate to the next slide's report using the "Next" button.
  4. Go back to the previous slide's report using the "Back" button.
